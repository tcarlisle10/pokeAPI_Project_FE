Pokémon Lore

Discover the fascinating stories and legends behind your favorite Pokémon!

Mewtwo

Mewtwo is a Psychic-type Legendary Pokémon. Created from the DNA of the mythical Pokémon Mew, it was engineered for battle. Mewtwo's powerful psychic abilities and its struggle against its own purpose make it one of the most compelling stories in Pokémon lore.

Fun Fact

Did you know? Pikachu was originally going to be a small, chubby creature called "Pikachu" that resembled a squirrel before evolving into the iconic Pokémon we know today!

Groudon

Groudon is a Ground-type Legendary Pokémon known as the "Continent Pokémon." It has the ability to expand continents and control volcanic eruptions. Its battle against Kyogre, the sea deity, symbolizes the clash between land and sea.

Lugia

Lugia is known as the "Diving Pokémon." Often referred to as the guardian of the seas, it has the power to calm storms and is said to have created the seas with its incredible power. Its presence is felt in many stories across the Pokémon world.

Legendary Birds

The Legendary Birds—Articuno, Zapdos, and Moltres—are known for their control over ice, electricity, and fire, respectively. They are often seen as symbols of balance in nature, and their legendary status makes them highly sought after by trainers.

Eevee Evolutions

Eevee is unique for its multiple evolutions, known as "Eeveelutions." Each evolution corresponds to a different element, allowing it to adapt and thrive in various environments, making it one of the most versatile Pokémon in the franchise.

About Pokémon
